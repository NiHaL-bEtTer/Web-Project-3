Implementation Of The Treaty Today

Today, Treaty 1 means Canada gives First Nations schools, health care and annual treaty payments. First Nations can also obtain urban land as an In the latter case it usually is a land in a city that the First Nation selects and the Federal government reserves the land, the road, water and other service arrangements are then made between governments. Those include Naawi Oodena in Winnipeg—where 160 acres of former military land was transferred as reserve land in December 2022 to make way for homes, shops and cultural centres—and Long Plain First Nations Madison urban reserve, home to their Petro Canada gas station at 490 Madison Street, which generates jobs and income for the community.
